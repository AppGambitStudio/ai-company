# CEO Brief — MRV Prototype

**Date:** 2026-04-02
**Source:** Dhaval Nagar (CEO), live session

---

Next Generation MRV (Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification) Prototype for the World Bank's Department for Climate Change.

Enables online collaboration on ISFL Monitoring Reports between:
- **World Bank (FMT)** — Forest Management Team
- **Participating Countries** — national-level reporting entities
- **Auditor Entities** — third-party verification bodies

Key context:
- ISFL = Initiative for Sustainable Forest Landscapes
- MRV = Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification (standard climate change accountability framework)
- This is a prototype / next-generation rebuild for the World Bank
- Multi-stakeholder collaborative reporting platform
